5.0 out of 5 stars Best soccer app December 30, 2012
By Campbell
Amazon Verified Purchase
This is probably the best soccer playing app there is. I love soccer and this app has great play. The daily challenge keeps you coming back and challenges you all the time. The graphics are amazing with the players looking relatively like their real life counterparts. There are tons of different leagues to play in and all of the teams. You can adjust many different parts of the game like viewing angle, controls, difficulty for games, and auto vs. manual settings. You can also manage your team and make subs and select player formation.

There are only a couple of down sides to FIFA 12. There is no career mode or manager careers. There also isn't a multiplayer option so you can't play with friends. The teams also aren't current, like in FIFA 13.

Aside from these things the game is amazing!!! If you're looking for a good soccer game, FIFA 12 is the obvious choice.
