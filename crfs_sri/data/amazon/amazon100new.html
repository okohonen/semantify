5.0 out of 5 starsVery good mid-level ballMay 5, 2012
By Noah M. Chafets
Size Name:5|Amazon Verified Purchase
I bought this after buying and then returning a $30 adidas ball, which was well reviewed but obviously very poorly made - the individual panels popped out at strange angles along the stitching, and the ball was lopsided. This nike ball - the second I've owned - is clearly a step up. The stitching and panel protrusion are very regular. The ball's weight and elasticity feel as they ought to (to someone who's been playing as an amateur for 20+ years), and the ball retains air about as it should. This is a great ball for the price. I'd buy it again in a heartbeat.

Nike premier team ball

I bought this thinking it would be an OK replacement for my Puma ball ($40). Was I wrong.
This ball is way better than I thought. I loved it so much I got nine of them for my whole
soccer team. Hand-stitched? First I thought YA right. It is amazing I have not even seen a single rip in them.

The craftsmanship is awesome. Geo balanced technology for accurate flight, sounded iffy but it is pretty good. The bladder has a six-wing latex for rebound and air retention.
And at 1.6 pounds its perfect for my team. The color is nice and simple.
I'll recommend this ball to anyone that has a soccer team from 8-14 yr.old.
this is the ball you want.
