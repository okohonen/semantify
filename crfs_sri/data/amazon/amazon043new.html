5.0 out of 5 starsPerfectly functional and the acrylic dome is not an issue for meDecember 14, 2012
By Mike TarraniHALL OF FAMETOP 50 REVIEWER
Up front I want to disclose that I purchased my set from a local store so I knew exactly what I was getting when I bought it.

What I love about this set is it's compact (everything can be stored under the dome), and it's a perfect match to my lifestyle and snacking habits.

As for storage, you can put your favorite antipasti in the four dishes, stack the cutting board on top of those - topped with some excellent cheeses or other snack items you would need to slice - and place the dome on top. This arrangement takes up a lot less space in a refrigerator than individual containers for each snack item, and when you are ready to serve all you have to do is remove the dome and set out the cutting board and dishes. The fork and cheese knife are a wonderful touch and are well made and very functional.

Since my snacks are usually olives, artichoke hearts, roasted peppers and the such the quarter dishes that come with this set are absolutely perfect. Each dish is also large enough to hold hummus and various sauces (salsa, bruschetta, etc.) for very small gatherings or individual snacking. The cutting board and cheese knife and fork make this set even more functional and practical.

All of the components are exceptionally well constructed - even the controversial acrylic dome. About the dome: I personally view it as a positive because I am forever dropping and breaking things and I doubt replacing the dome would be easy if it were made of glass. As it is the thing is practically indestructible. As another reviewer has noted the dome is set aside when you are serving snacks with the set anyway, so it should be a non-issue. If you scratch the dome it's easily restored to like-new condition with products such as NOVUS 7136 Plastic Polish Kit - 2 oz.

My only complaint is the current price of this set, which I think if a tad high. Mine cost a third less locally and I am not sure I would have shelled out the nearly forty bucks it's selling for here at the time of this review (but it is near Christmas and the price make have recently increased).

If you can get past the acrylic dome this is a wonderful set if you entertain small groups or snack on something healthier than chips and other junk food when watching TV. Mine has become so handy that I would purchase another if something were to happen to it. Read more
