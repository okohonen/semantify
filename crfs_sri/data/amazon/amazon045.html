Product Description

Size: 15"

This Mariposa cheese set from Picnic Time features a two-toned gourmet cheese board with wine and cheese accessories tucked away into two swing-out drawers. This piece is made of light and dark bamboo, a stunning visual combination, and measures 15 by 9 by 2 inches. The stainless steel cheese tools include: one hard cheese knife, one chisel knife for hard crumbly cheese, and one cheese fork. For the wine tools, we have included a stainless steel waiter-style corkscrew, drip ring, and bottle stopper. Simply add your favorite vino, yummy cheese and crackers and you have all the components for a fine evening.

2.0 out of 5 stars Not worth the money. December 4, 2012
By Erozin84
Size Name:15"|Amazon Verified Purchase
After browsing amazon's selection of the alike, - price, quality, craftsmanship, and functionality seemingly became one when I came across the Picnic Time Mariposa cheese board/tool set. So I decided to order one for my father as a Christmas gift. My dad is 100% Italiano - so let's just say he really loves his cheese (and pepperoni) snacks.

Well, I just received it in the mail today and I am utterly disappointed. I was actually really looking forward to opening up the package because of how wonderful the set looked online. But here are the issues I have with it:

1. The outer trim/compartments are NOT a darker finish as shown in the picture. Not necessarily a biggie, just be aware of that.
2. One of the underside swiveling compartments (that holds the wine tools, in particular) was loose/felt flimsy and was overtly drooping. This defect, or what have you, automatically made this set look like it was broken, and therefore, not gift worthy.
3. Both of the underside compartments seem to "flop" around, even when only slightly tilting the board. This was just more of an annoyance than anything else.

Conclusively, this product received some great reviews, which played a big factor in my decision to purchase it. So perhaps I received a defective cheese board... or maybe I'm just another stubborn Italiano like my dad (but we'll stick with the first theory). Either way, I would avoid this one. In my opinion, and minus the issues I have with it, it's still not even worth the money. So do yourself a favor, kids - "forget about it". I'm going to return this and get a different one.

3.0 out of 5 stars Nice, but mine was defective August 24, 2013
By PugLuvr
Size Name:15"|Amazon Verified Purchase
I saw this cheese board and liked it right away because it was a bigger size than most, a cute shape, and had the wine tools in addition to the cheese tools. All the reviews were great except one, that complained that the swivel unit holding the tools was too loose. I ordered it anyway, a gift for my daughter and her husband's 'wood' (5th) anniversary. When it arrived, I found the same issue the previous reviewer found... one of the swivel compartments was WAAAY too loose, you could see more than a half inch of screw with it hanging down. There is no apparent way to tighten it, the screw is imbedded in the rest of the structure. The other compartment was fine, but with the issue it had, I returned it and ordered a different item. Also, it's made in China. If it hadn't had the defect, I would have been happy with it, the tools are of nice quality, and the wood seems nice. I would have taken a replacement, but would have had to wait 3-4 weeks, and that was too long.
