1.0 out of 5 starsWorks badly with existing BL-C series camerasMarch 7, 2013
By Friedman Wagner-Dobler
Amazon Verified Purchase
The BL-series of Panasonic cameras was a serviceable product - nothing spectacular, bot nothing really bad either. The VT164 is an epic fail. I imagine at some point they decided to make sure that it was not going to be useful for serious or commercial use - so they removed the PoE feature: now you have to run a network cable and a power cable. Ugh. And if you already have BL-C series cameras? They won't integrate cleanly: you can't have a BL-C series multi-camera display, with one of the cameras being a VT164 (it will show up as a thumbnail, but clicking on it will give you an error 404). The supplied software forces you to install a Windows client to get multiple cameras. If you're only intending to use one camera, or if you're starting afresh and don't mind installing client software, this might meet your needs. Otherwise it's a monument to a spectacular case of product management hubris.

5.0 out of 5 starsExcellent camera for sub 200January 27, 2013
By Jim
Amazon Verified Purchase
I understand why people aren't happy with this product specially those who previously owned Panasonic IP cameras. The interface of this software is completely different . Unlike the previous generation cameras, this camera requires you to install the software before viewing . Sadly, Panasonic does not alert people if they haven't installed the software (as the result, I see a lot of reviewer here who complain that the software does not work).

Mine works beautifully after I install the software as instructed by the manually. I can remotely control and view my camera using IP Cam Soft Pro app (available on iTunes) .

Pros:
Good video quality in the day time
Small, nice looking
Work great with Iphone/Android app (IP Cam Soft Pro)

Cons:
Does not have IR led light (So, night video is not very good)
Unlike previous model, no POE
