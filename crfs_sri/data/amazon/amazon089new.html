    Bulky and poor quality.
    By J. Hyne on January 29, 2011
    Amazon Verified Purchase
    I'm a very unusual size: 15.5" collar, 37" sleeve, with a 32/34" waist - i.e. slim & tall. I thought I had found an incredible deal here with this shirt, which I had ordered specifically for a holiday party. Not only is the price good, but it appears that it's available in my size: win/win.

    Upon receiving the shirt I was very disappointed and decided to wear a shirt that I already own. The cut, which is listed as 'fitted' is baggy and loose. If you're thinking this is a slim-fit shirt, think again. Additionally, the material feels cheap and uninviting. I wouldn't recommend purchasing this shirt for anything other than work, and then only if you're not dealing with clients.

    I decided to return it, my first return ever from Amazon - which I'm happy to say has been stress free thus far.

    Quality matters with clothing and it's worth spending a bit more for something that you're happy wearing - something that will last. For slim fits I would recommend your looking into Charles Tyrwhitt's shirts. They sometimes have amazing sales and I am more than pleased with the shirts that I have had delivered by them thus far.

    Looks good, comfortable, but snags on things
    By Nick Stewart on October 26, 2010
    Amazon Verified Purchase
    This shirt looks good, and feels great to wear. It's comfortable and breathes better than many dress shirts. I've gone through two of them, they last as good as any shirt I've had, but I did notice as it got a bit old the color faded slightly.

    One bad thing is that the sharp looking satin sripes (which are slightly raised to give a nice looking texture to the shirt) tend to snag on things. The slightest touch against any sharp corner or rough surface and the stripes will snag and pull out a big loop of thread. Very annoying. Once I learned this I was careful not to snag on things, but the first couple times it happened I was really steamed. If you only work in an office this might not be an issue, but if you do anything physical in your job it can be irritating.
